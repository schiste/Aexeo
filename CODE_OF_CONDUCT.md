# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Assume good intent, but challenge technical claims clearly.
- Focus criticism on code, design, and decisions, not people.
- Respect differing experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Deliberate disruption of discussion or review
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately. Reports should be handled promptly and with discretion.
